About

Born in India, living in Ireland, and still figuring out if cricket or GAA deserves more passion. I landed in marketing because it is the one field where storytelling and strategy share the same sentence.

On the career side, I have spent the last few years building full-funnel marketing programmes for online learning platforms. Writing SEO microsites, email drip sequences, award entries, and press releases, often in the same week. I cover the full marketing mix and genuinely enjoy it.

Outside work you will find me at the gym, watching a match, or convincing myself that planning a trip counts as actually taking one. Same energy in campaigns: structured thinking, bias for action, and a strong view on what good copy actually means.

How I Work

My shift to AI-first workflows

Over the last year I have been deliberately rebuilding how I work. Not by replacing thinking with AI, but by using it to compress the distance between brief and output. Research that used to take a day now takes an hour. First drafts that would sit overnight get iterated in real time.

The result is that I can run more campaigns, test more angles, and produce more content without scaling headcount. In a marketing function where speed and quality both matter, that is a genuine edge.

I use AI as a thought partner, a drafting assistant, and a research accelerant. Strategy, judgment, and final quality control stay with me.

Drafting and Iteration

Claude and ChatGPT for copy drafting, brief interpretation, content iteration, and messaging refinement across all asset types.

Daily Trends and Topic Research

Using Perplexity and AI-powered search to track industry trends, competitor moves, and content opportunities every morning, feeding directly into content calendars and campaign angles.

SEO Research at Speed

AI-assisted keyword clustering, SERP analysis, and content gap identification. Research cycles cut from days to hours.

Email Personalisation at Scale

Generating copy variation across persona segments without producing generic content, sequenced and structured for each ICP.

Custom Marketing Tools

Built and deployed custom AI-powered workflows using the Claude API, Zoho, and Zapier to automate research, reporting, and content production cycles.
